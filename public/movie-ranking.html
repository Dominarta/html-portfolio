The Best Movies According to LG

My top three movies of all-time

Along Came Polly

Some of the humour in Along Came Polly is a little much for me, but the Ben Stiller/Jennifer Aniston bond makes up for it, though you can debate how believable a pairing they are.

Rocky

Ever since it first came out in 1976 and subsequently won the Academy Award for Best Picture in 1977, Rocky developed a sort of reputation as one of the greatest sports films of all time.

Casino Royale

Casino Royale is one of the most iconic James Bond movies, as it features a star-studded cast, beautiful locations, and of course, the intriguing casino world!
